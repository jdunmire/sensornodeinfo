RockingD Labs

RockingD Labs is the moniker I use for my projects, experiments, and explorations as an engineer and a maker.

This site documents devices, software, and network I am using to implement a network of sensors for my house. I know, that is not a very unique project. Still, I haven't found what I'm looking for in other designs and for me it is fun and rewarding excercise.

If you find the information here useful or would like to contribute (even if it is only comments, suggestions, or pointing out mistakes), I would be happy to hear from you. Just use the button below (for now the Us is just a pretension).
