I am using Google Analytics on this site. That means that information about your visit to this site is being collected unless you take steps to opt-out (see below).

What is Google Analytics?

Google Analytics is a widely used service from Google that grabs data about visitors to this site (you) and provides summaries of that information. A few of the things Google Analytics records are:

  • The website you came from to get here.
  • How long you spent looking at this site.
  • The type of computer and browser you are using.

Google Analytics reports website trends without identifying individual visitors.

What do I do with your data?

The tracking information allows me to determine how many people visit this site and what pages they find most interesting.

Google Analytics provides this information as statistical data about visitors browsing actions and patterns. The information and reports do not identify individuals.

My activity falls within the bounds of the Google Analytics Terms of Service .
